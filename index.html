Case Study

Nonprofit Website Redesign:
Portland Metro Beekeeping Association

The Portland Metro Beekeepers Association (PMBA) relies on donations, membership, and volunteers to provide resources to the community on bees and beekeeping, however, it competes with multiple local beekeeping associations and may not be attracting new members or website interest.

The Solution

Increase interest in the PMBA website by designing a space that has a clear mission, lots of resources, and feels welcoming to users and potential future members.

My Role

User Research, Information Architecture, Interface Design, Testing

Background

PMBA is a volunteer-run Portland Beekeeping Association that has been meeting since 1990, and is committed to sharing their knowledge and expertise around all things bee with others, by offering resources and information to help people of all skill levels learn more about bees and beekeeping.
